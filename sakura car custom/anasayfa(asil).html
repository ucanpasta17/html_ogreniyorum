Who Are We ?

Sakura is a special world that combines our passion for Japanese car culture with modern aesthetics. Born in Kusadasi, this dream was founded for those who see the art of tuning as a way of life, not just an occupation.

We believe that every vehicle has a soul.
At Sakura, we leave the ordinary behind and shape your car according to your character. From JDM enthusiasts to drift enthusiasts, from simple elegance to bold designs, we have a touch for everyone.

We design with craftsmanship and apply with love.
Handcrafted details, special interior designs with pastel tones, LED lighting, and Japanese-style exterior body kits, we create your car from scratch. For us, modification means power of expression before speed.

Sakura is not a workshop, it's a community.
Every customer is also part of our team. Every vehicle we meet on the road becomes a story we write together.

Sakura is not just a garage - it's a community of people who share the same dream. From tuners who are devoted to JDM culture to designers who admire pastel aesthetics, everyone lives their own style here. Here, ideas are spoken freely, each vehicle tells a story.

Members of our community share their designs, discuss the construction stages and get inspired by each other with each new project. Whether you're designing detailed body kits or making a difference with interior lighting - Sakura listens and supports you.

Every meeting is a sharing, every project a partnership. We come together at our events: car shows, co-modification days and gatherings inspired by Japanese street culture to learn and have fun. We get to know each other on the road. Every vehicle bearing the Sakura label is a part of this community, a symbol of a shared dream.
